When you grow into more senior roles like Ellen, your responsibilities change. They grow, and dependencies increase over time, calendar overflows, and to-do lists become endless. ## How to Get Better at Prioritization

Since you will become the team representative in senior and staff roles, you have to learn stakeholder management to bring more clarity to work. Understanding which stakeholder should have the higher priority in a project is critical for alignment in all other projects.
