## Building with AI

I subscribed to Claude Max (💸) to test the waters during my vacation and create an application for myself that I wanted for years. I had a $20 subscription but I hit its limit in 15 minutes 😅. Damn Claude, you're expensive. I still feel uncomfortable to lock myself to Claude ecosystem. However, it's super comfortable. I can practically develop an application while walking my dog. What I'm missing is testing other models. Luckily, I have access to other models at work.

Anyway, I'm full into the building mode with Claude. It feels amazing. I now can send blog articles from my phone with a newly built custom app. I will use it to feed my [Journal](/journal) section more with shorter journal-like posts on my progress with all the things written on this page.

## Theater

In the last six months, I have been trying to continue my journey in Improv Theater. I am finishing the third level of Improv, which focuses on Patterns and Repetition. I must say this level is difficult. This is my fourth class (Level 1, Level 2.1 and 2.2) and oh boy, I feel awkward during the class because I don't get it. But whatever, it's still fun to push my brain into something unrelated to technology. It's still way outside of my comfort zone and I constantly fail. In every class, there are so many moments where I fail. It's good to learn how that feels. It sucks. But it's still fun because you know what: there is nothing at stake!

As a next step, before continuing the level 4, I decided to take a slight detour and signed up for a class for scripted acting! It's the first level class and I think it will be fun!

This hobby, learning how to act, how to be spontaneous, and how to create a character, stay in it and abstract it from yourself blows my mind sometimes. I love every piece of it. If you're in Berlin, let me know and I'm happy to talk about this hobby and maybe we perform something together? Why not make ourselves a little sillier together?
